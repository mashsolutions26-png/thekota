About The Kota

Born to Create, Raised to Entertain

Born and raised in the historic city of Polonnaruwa, Sri Lanka, Madhusha Nethmal started his YouTube journey in 2019. What began as a passion for creating content quickly evolved into one of Sri Lanka's most beloved gaming and entertainment channels.

With a unique blend of meme reviews, gaming content, and reactions, The Kota has captured the hearts of thousands of fans across the island and beyond. His authentic personality and entertaining commentary have made him a household name in Sri Lankan YouTube culture.
